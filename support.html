Getting Started

After installing SMS Cleaner+, follow these steps to set up message filtering:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Go to Messages
  3. Tap Unknown & Spam
  4. Enable SMS Cleaner+ by toggling the switch

Once enabled, SMS Cleaner+ will automatically help filter unwanted messages from unknown senders.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does SMS Cleaner+ store my messages?

No, SMS Cleaner+ does not store or share any of your SMS data. We use Apple's Message Filter Extension technology which processes messages directly on your device. Your messages never leave your phone.

Q: Why do I still see spam messages?

Make sure SMS Cleaner+ is properly enabled in your iOS settings. Go to Settings → Messages → Unknown & Spam and verify that SMS Cleaner+ is toggled on. Keep in mind that no filter is 100% accurate, but we continuously work to improve detection rates.

Q: How can I disable SMS filtering?

To disable filtering, go to Settings → Messages → Unknown & Spam and toggle off SMS Cleaner+. You can re-enable it at any time using the same steps.
